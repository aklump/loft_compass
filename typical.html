Loft compass extends the blueprint framework providing up to 15 breakpoints (viewports) and grid widths. It simplifies grids and media queries by thinking in terms of devices.

Resize your browser window to see the various viewports. For best results use a browser, such as Opera, that allows you to reduce your window width to 0px. You should study the SASS files in this source code to learn this framework.
